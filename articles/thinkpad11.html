ThinkPad 11... in 2026

I was looking for a laptop a few months ago because every single one that I buy loves to do this silly thing called "break immediately after I buy it". My last laptops were (in backwards chronological order): a very slow HP Pavillion with an i3 processor (died of "I broke the battery cable"), another older HP Pavillion (still alive, but painfully slow), and a ThinkPad Yoga 11e (died of CMOS corruption, which happens to be a common problem with them).

So a few months ago, I hopped on eBay and bought myself a ThinkPad 11 from 2016 for around $80.
